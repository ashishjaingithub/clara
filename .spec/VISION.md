# Clara — Product Vision

Clara is the AI receptionist that local SMBs never had time to build themselves. Every small business loses customers to missed calls, unanswered questions, and the gap between when customers need help and when a human is available — Clara closes that gap with a chat interface that knows the business, answers instantly, and hands off to a human when the question is real. The product starts as a sales asset: when a Hunter outreach email lands in a prospect's inbox, the demo link Clara generates shows them their own AI receptionist before any sales call, eliminating the imagination gap that kills every other B2B SaaS demo.

The long-term vision is Clara embedded on every SMB website — not as a generic chatbot but as an AI receptionist that actually knows this salon's stylists, this dental office's insurance policies, this restaurant's seasonal menu. The wedge is zero setup: Hunter's data enrichment pre-configures Clara so an SMB owner never fills out a form. The moat is the knowledgebase — a shared, owner-maintained source of truth that both Clara (chat) and Veya (voice) draw from, so every customer touchpoint gives the same accurate, up-to-date answer regardless of whether they typed or called.
